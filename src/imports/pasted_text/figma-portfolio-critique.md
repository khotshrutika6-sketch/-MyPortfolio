## 🟡 2. Visual hierarchy (BIG issue)

Right now everything feels:

* Same size
* Same weight
* Same importance

### ❌ Result:

User doesn’t know where to look

### ✅ Fix:

Use clear hierarchy:

* H1 → 40–48px (Name)
* H2 → 24–32px (Section titles)
* Body → 16–18px

👉 Good portfolios guide the eye clearly ([Figma][2])

---

## 🟡 3. Projects section (MOST IMPORTANT 🔥)

This is where your portfolio is weak.

### ❌ What you're likely doing:

* Just showing cards/screenshots

### ❌ Why that’s bad:

Hiring managers don’t care about UI only.

---

### ✅ What you MUST add to each project:

Inside each project:

* Problem → What was the challenge?
* Your role → What did YOU do?
* Process → Design / development steps
* Result → Outcome (very important)

👉 Strong portfolios use storytelling, not just visuals ([Figma][2])
